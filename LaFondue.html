LA FONDUE

This project rebrands for a traditional and famous Swiss dish like Cheese fondue and chocolate fondue, with most popular grilled styled fondue.

OVERVIEW

La Fondue is located in the center of Saratoga Village on Big Basin Way at the corner of 4th Street. The restaurant’s interior reflects a new interpretation of European design that avoids cliché interpretations. La Fondue offers a unique fondue experience serving over 50 different types of fondues served table-side.

BUSINESS ANALYSIS

This is a restaurant located in Saratoga, near west of San Jose. It is a Swiss restaurant. It serves traditional and famous Swiss dish like Cheese fondue and chocolate fondue, with most popular grilled styled fondue. The price is not cheap, but I think it worths the price.

The environment is awesome. The restaurant is located in a beautiful and quiet town. Inside the restaurant, tables are not very close to each other, and the light is not very bright. So, when we were dinning there, we did not feel crowed, and each group were kept in a reasonable distance, which is very comfortable. On each table, there is either a small lamp or a small candle, which make everyone feel relaxed. I love the environment and food in this restaurant, and the service there is super. We never need to ask for something, because waiters there are super thoughtful and nice, they provide us whatever we want in a timely manner.

BUSINESS ANALYSIS

Things I do not love, when doing the grill on the table, we overcooked, and make the pan a little bit dirty, while we find no way to clean it.

This restaurant give me a feel that I was well served and being treated as a very important customer. It is like a traditional Europe restaurant, but combined with some United States country style (because it located in a quiet suburb.)
