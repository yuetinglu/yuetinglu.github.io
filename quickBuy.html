QUICK BUY

Quick buy can tell you fastest way to get item (online or onsite) and where to buy them.

WORDLIST

MISSION

Shopping is an important and necessary part of our lives. Although online shopping will bring convenience to people, online shopping requires people to take the time to wait. Sometimes you need to get something right away, but you can't find it quickly. Then you need a shopping assistant to help you get what you want immediately. Quick buy can tell you fastest way to get item (online or onsite) and where to buy them.

PROBLEM

I often shopping on Amazon.com, although some goods can be delivered at the same day. But for most of them we need to wait for one to two days. If you want to buy something immediately and often need go several stores to find what I need, it wastes a lot of time. I believe that many people have such problems, so I came out of this idea.

RATIONAL

It is a website that combines online and physical stores.

It is a website that can helps you get the commodity in shortest time.

It is a website that you can get everything in one website.

It is a website that can helps you save time on shopping.
